// Overview

ABOUT NUMBER TO WORDS

The Number to Words Converter translates any numeric value into its English word equivalent. This is essential for cheques (amounts must be written in words and numerals), legal documents, invoices, and financial statements where amounts are spelled out to prevent tampering or ambiguity.

India uses a unique numbering system with lakhs (1,00,000) and crores (1,00,00,000) that differs from the international million/billion system. Our converter supports both, making it useful for domestic Indian use (writing Rs amounts on cheques) and international use (writing USD amounts in global documents).

// Examples

  • 1,000,000 = One Million (international)
