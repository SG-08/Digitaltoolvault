// Overview

ABOUT THE CURRENCY CONVERTER

Our Currency Converter fetches live exchange rates to give accurate, up-to-date currency conversions. India is the world's largest remittance recipient — the Indian diaspora in UAE, USA, UK, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, and Canada regularly convert between INR and local currencies for salary transfers, savings, and investments.

Exchange rates fluctuate continuously based on RBI monetary policy, trade balance, global economic conditions, and geopolitical events. Always check the latest rate before making large forex transactions or international wire transfers.

The converter supports 150+ currencies via the ExchangeRate API. Common use cases include NRI salary conversions, international travel budgeting, freelancer USD/EUR earnings in INR, import/export business planning, and shopping on foreign websites.

// India Forex Tips

FOREX TIPS FOR INDIA

  • Forex cards offer better rates than credit cards or cash for international travel
  • Avoid airport forex — rates are 5-8% worse than market rate
  • Under RBI LRS you can remit up to USD 2,50,000 per financial year
  • Remittances above Rs 7 lakh attract 20% TCS — claim it back when filing ITR
