INTRODUCTION

Welcome to DigitalToolVault ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website located at www.digitaltoolvault.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using this Site, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the Site.

// Data Collection

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1. Tool Input Data: All tools on DigitalToolVault (calculators, converters, image tools, etc.) run entirely within your browser. Input values such as loan amounts, age, income, or uploaded images are processed locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

2. Usage Data (Analytics): We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, device type, browser type, and approximate geographic location (at the country/region level). This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

3. Contact Form Data: If you contact us via our Contact page, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without explicit consent.

4. Cookies: See our Cookie section below for a detailed breakdown of how cookies are used on this Site.

COOKIES & TRACKING

We use the following types of cookies on this Site:

  • Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics): These cookies help us understand how users interact with our site — which tools are most popular, which pages have high bounce rates, and how users navigate. This data helps us improve the platform. Google Analytics data is anonymised and does not personally identify individual users.

  • No Tracking of Tool Inputs: We do not place any cookies that capture or transmit the data you enter into our tools.

Managing Cookies: You can control and/or delete cookies from your browser settings at any time. You may also opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.
