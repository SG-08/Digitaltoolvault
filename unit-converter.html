// Overview

ABOUT THE UNIT CONVERTER

The Unit Converter handles length, weight, temperature, area, and speed conversions between metric (SI) and imperial units. India officially uses the metric system but informal use of imperial units (feet/inches for height, pounds in some communities) is widespread, making unit conversion a frequent everyday need.

Temperature conversion is important for cooking (Fahrenheit in imported recipes), medicine (body temperature), and weather (international forecasts). Area conversion is critical for real estate — properties are listed in square feet, square yards, acres, or square metres depending on the region and seller.

// Common Conversions

EVERYDAY CONVERSIONS

// FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How many cm in 1 foot?
1 foot = 30.48 cm.
What is 100C in Fahrenheit?
(100 x 9/5) + 32 = 212F — boiling point of water.
How many kg in 1 pound?
1 lb = 0.4536 kg. 1 kg = 2.2046 lbs.
What is 1 acre in sq feet?
1 acre = 43,560 sq feet = 4,047 sq metres.
