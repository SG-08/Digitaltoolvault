Frequently Asked Questions

How many km in a mile?1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. So 10 miles = 16.09 km and 100 km = 62.14 miles.
How to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. So 37°C (body temperature) = (37 × 1.8) + 32 = 98.6°F.
How many grams in a pound?1 pound = 453.592 grams. 1 kg = 2.20462 pounds.
