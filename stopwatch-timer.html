// Overview

ABOUT STOPWATCH AND TIMER

Our Stopwatch and Countdown Timer combine two essential time-tracking tools on one page. The Stopwatch measures elapsed time to the second with unlimited lap tracking. The Countdown Timer counts down from any duration (hours, minutes, seconds) and shows DONE when it reaches zero.

Both tools run entirely in your browser — accessible on any device without downloading an app. Simply bookmark the page for quick access anytime.

Common uses in India: timing biryani and pressure cooker cooking (exact minutes matter), exam practice papers (build time discipline), Pomodoro productivity sessions (25-minute work blocks), HIIT workout intervals, yoga and meditation timing, and keeping meetings within scheduled durations.
