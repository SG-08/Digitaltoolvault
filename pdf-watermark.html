PDF Watermark – Add Text Watermark to PDF Documents Free

Add custom text watermarks to your PDF documents online for free. Mark PDFs as Confidential, Draft, Copy or any custom text — no software needed, works in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. No signup required.

Why Watermark PDF Documents?

Adding a watermark to a PDF communicates the document's status at a glance and provides a layer of intellectual property protection. Common reasons to watermark a PDF include marking draft versions, protecting proprietary information, branding official documents and deterring unauthorised printing or distribution.

PDF Watermark Tips

  • Diagonal placement — a diagonal watermark is harder to crop out and harder to ignore
  • 40–50% opacity — visible without obscuring the document content completely
  • Large font size — for a single-word watermark, use a large font to cover the page
  • Consistent styling — match watermark colour to your brand (e.g. company colour)
💡 For legal documents, a watermark alone does not make a document legally binding or prevent copying. Use document encryption (password protection) in addition to watermarking for truly sensitive files.
