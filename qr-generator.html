// Overview

ABOUT QR CODES

A QR code is a 2D barcode that smartphones can scan to instantly access encoded information — URLs, payment links, contact details, WiFi credentials, or plain text. QR codes are ubiquitous in India: on UPI payment receipts, restaurant menus, product packaging, event tickets, and business cards.

Our generator creates scannable QR codes using the QR Server API. Generated codes work with Google Lens, Paytm, PhonePe, BHIM, and the native camera app on all modern Android and iOS devices. All generated QR codes are permanent — the information is encoded directly in the image and does not require our servers to decode.

// What to Encode

WHAT YOU CAN ENCODE

  • Website URL: Share links without typing
  • UPI payment: upi://pay?pa=yourID@bank&pn=Name — customers scan to pay
  • WiFi: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;
  • Phone: tel:+919876543210 — tap to call
  • Email: mailto:email@example.com

// Tips

QR CODE TIPS

  • Test before printing — scan with multiple devices
  • Dark code on white background for best scanning
  • Leave white space (quiet zone) around the QR
  • Shorter URLs make simpler (easier to scan) QR codes
