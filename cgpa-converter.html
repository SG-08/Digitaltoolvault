Understanding CGPA and Percentage

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) represents overall academic performance on a scale of 10, while percentage represents marks out of 100. Most Indian institutions follow the standard formula:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

For example, if your CGPA is 8.0, your percentage would be 8.0 × 9.5 = 76%. This simple formula helps convert grades quickly and accurately.
