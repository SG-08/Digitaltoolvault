Understanding CGPA and Percentage

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a grading system used by many educational institutions to measure overall academic performance on a scale of 10. Percentage, on the other hand, represents marks out of 100.

In most Indian universities and boards, the standard formula used is:
Percentage = CGPA × 9.5
