Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Roman numeral for 2025?2025 in Roman numerals is MMXXV. M=1000, M=1000, X=10, X=10, V=5.
Why is 4 written as IV and not IIII?IV uses the subtractive notation — placing a smaller numeral before a larger one means subtract. IV = 5 − 1 = 4. This rule applies to IX (9), XL (40), XC (90), CD (400), CM (900).
What is the largest Roman numeral?The standard Roman numeral system goes up to 3,999 (MMMCMXCIX). Numbers beyond this require special notations not covered in standard usage.
