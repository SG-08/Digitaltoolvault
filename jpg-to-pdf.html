// Overview

ABOUT JPG TO PDF

The JPG to PDF Converter transforms JPG or PNG images into PDF documents entirely in your browser. PDF is the universal format for official submissions. Converting photos to PDF is essential when submitting scanned documents to government portals, banks, colleges, and employers who require PDF format.

Common India use cases: converting scanned Aadhaar, PAN, or mark sheets to PDF for portal uploads, combining multiple scanned document pages into one PDF, and submitting scanned bills and receipts in PDF format.

Your images are never uploaded to our servers — all conversion is local in your browser, critical for sensitive documents like identity proofs and financial records.

TIPS
