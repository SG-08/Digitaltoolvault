Step-Up SIP Calculator – Plan Your Future Wealth Smartly

The Step-Up SIP Calculator helps you estimate your investment growth by increasing your SIP every year. It is ideal for investors who expect their income to grow and want to maximize returns.

What is Step-Up SIP?

Step-Up SIP is a strategy where you increase your monthly investment annually. This allows you to build a larger corpus compared to a fixed SIP.

Key Benefits

  • Higher returns than regular SIP
  • Adapts with salary growth
  • Better inflation management
  • Disciplined investing

How to Use

  • Add expected return
  • Select investment duration
  • Choose step-up option
