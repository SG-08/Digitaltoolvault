Step-Up SIP Calculator – Plan Your Investment Growth Easily

This Step-Up SIP calculator helps you estimate how your investments grow over time when you increase your SIP contribution every year. It is one of the best tools for long-term wealth creation and smart financial planning.

What is Step-Up SIP?

Step-Up SIP is a strategy where your investment amount increases annually. This allows your investments to grow faster and generate higher returns compared to a fixed SIP.

How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your monthly SIP amount
  • Enter expected return rate
  • Select investment duration
  • Enter step-up percentage or amount
  • View your final wealth instantly

Benefits of Step-Up SIP

  • Higher wealth creation
  • Beats inflation
  • Aligns with income growth
  • Better long-term planning

Example

If you start with ₹10,000 SIP and increase it every year, your final investment value can be significantly higher than a fixed SIP.
