Discount Formula

Sale Price = Original × (1 − Discount% ÷ 100)
Savings = Original − Sale Price

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the original price from a discounted price?Original Price = Sale Price ÷ (1 − Discount% ÷ 100). If you paid ₹600 after 25% off, original = 600 ÷ 0.75 = ₹800.
What is double discount?A double discount applies two discounts sequentially. 20% then 10% off is NOT 30% off — it's: price × 0.80 × 0.90 = 28% total discount.
How to calculate cashback discount?Cashback reduces the effective price after purchase. Effective price = Sale Price − Cashback Amount. Factor in cashback caps and wallet restrictions before calculating.
