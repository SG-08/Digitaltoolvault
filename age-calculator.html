// Overview

WHAT IS THE AGE CALCULATOR?

The Age Calculator is an online tool that computes your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth. Unlike a simple mental calculation that tells you only your age in years, our calculator gives you a precise breakdown including months and days, the total number of days you've lived, and how many days remain until your next birthday.

Age calculation is more complex than it appears — months have different lengths, leap years add an extra day, and determining the exact number of months and days requires careful date arithmetic. Our calculator handles all of this automatically, giving you accurate results for any date.

Beyond simple curiosity, precise age calculation is practically important for retirement planning (knowing exact age for NPS, EPF withdrawal), insurance policies, eligibility for government schemes (some have age cut-offs to the day), legal purposes, and school/college admissions.

// Guide

HOW TO USE THIS TOOL

  1. Enter your date of birth — select from the date picker (DD/MM/YYYY format).
  2. Enter "Calculate as of" date — leave blank to calculate age as of today, or enter a specific date to calculate age on that date.
  3. Click "Calculate Age" — instantly see your age in years/months/days, total days lived, and days to next birthday.

// FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I calculate age if I only know the birth year?
If you don't know the exact birth date, enter January 1 of the birth year as an approximation. The result will be approximate (±1 year).
Does this calculator handle leap years?
Yes. Our calculator correctly accounts for February 29 in leap years when computing exact days and months.
Can I calculate someone else's age?
Absolutely. Just enter their date of birth. There's no restriction on whose age you calculate.
What is the maximum age I can calculate?
Our calculator can handle dates from the early 1900s to present day without issue.
Is this useful for NPS or EPF withdrawal age checks?
Yes. The NPS retirement age is 60, and our calculator can tell you exactly when you'll reach that age (and how many days remain).
