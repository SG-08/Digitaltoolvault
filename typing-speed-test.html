Frequently Asked Questions

What is WPM?WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated by dividing the number of correctly typed words by the time taken in minutes. Standard WPM counts every 5 characters as one word.
How can I improve my typing speed?Practice touch typing with all 10 fingers in the home row position (ASDF JKL;). Keep eyes on the screen, not the keyboard. Practice for 15–20 minutes daily and speed naturally increases within weeks.
What is a good typing speed for jobs?Most office jobs require 40+ WPM. Data entry jobs typically require 60+ WPM. Customer support roles often require 50+ WPM. Medical transcription requires 70–80+ WPM.
Why does accuracy matter more than speed?Errors slow you down because you need to correct them. A typist at 70 WPM with 95% accuracy is more productive than one at 90 WPM with 80% accuracy. Always prioritise accuracy — speed follows.
