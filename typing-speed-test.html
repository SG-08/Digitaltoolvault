Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good typing speed?40–60 WPM is considered good for everyday use. Most office jobs require 40+ WPM. Data entry jobs typically require 60+ WPM. Top competitive typists reach 120–150 WPM.
How can I improve my typing speed?Practice touch typing — use all 10 fingers, keep eyes on screen (not keyboard). Use home row position (ASDF JKL;). Practice daily for 15–20 minutes. Sites like Typing.com and Keybr offer structured lessons.
What is accuracy in typing?Accuracy is the percentage of correctly typed characters. 95%+ accuracy is considered good. Prioritise accuracy over speed — speed naturally increases with consistent accurate practice.
